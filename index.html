INDY-1-RED-BookDatingApp

Overview:

The Book Selector app is a mobile application designed to make the book discovery process both engaging and personalized by employing a user-friendly, swipe-based interface inspired by dating apps like Hinge, Bumble, and Tinder. While current book recommendation systems limit their suggestions to books that are similar to ones that are being currently viewed, the Book Selector App offers a fresh alternative by tailoring recommendations to individual readers’ preferences.

Our goal is to provide readers with a fun, visually appealing way to explore new books while also alleviating the often overwhelming experience of finding books in a sea of endless options. The Book Selector app introduces a fresh, interactive approach to book discovery with a modern design that enhances the user experience. Users can swipe left to dismiss books or right to add them to their TBR (to-be-read) list. A tap reveals rich details like genre, tropes, descriptions, etc...

By combining an upgraded aesthetic with user-driven algorithms, the app not only enhances the book discovery process but also creates a unique way for authors and publishers to connect with their audiences. Put simply, the Book Selector app is not a replacement for Kindle or Goodreads but is instead an exciting companion app for discovering books that truly resonate with readers' tastes.
